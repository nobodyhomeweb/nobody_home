The Cosmoscope is a digital archival tool dedicated to preservation, mapping ideas and relationships rather than locations. It conceptualizes the archive as an interconnected web, visualizing complex interactions between social, cultural, ecological, economic, and political phenomena. As an alternative form of exploring and understanding our world, the Cosmoscope invites users to navigate knowledge through layered connections, unveiling patterns hidden within collective memory and discourse.
